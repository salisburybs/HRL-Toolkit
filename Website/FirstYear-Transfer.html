First-Year and Transfer Students

Congratulations on your admission to URI! We are delighted you have decided to become a part of the URI community. Step 1: Pay Deposits

Incoming first-year and transfer students must pay the Enrollment Deposit with Housing.  Please allow three business days for these payments to be processed by URI.  If you have any questions about the Enrollment Deposit please contact URI Admission at admission@uri.edu.

Step 2: Complete Housing Application in E-Campus

Once you have been accepted to URI, registered for e-Campus access, and paid the Enrollment Deposit with Housing, you may complete the application process by visiting Student e-Campus. Once logged into e-Campus:
  1. Log into MyHousing;
  2. Select the “Applications” tab;
  3. Select and complete the “First-Year Student Application” or "Transfer Student Application";
  4. Finally, follow the link provided to Dining Services and complete and submit a Resident Student Meal Plan application.
Students who miss the application deadline will not receive a guarantee of on-campus housing in one of the University’s residences.

Step 3:  (optional) Request Roommates

Once you have completed the housing application, you can log back in to search for/request other first-year students as roommates, if they have completed their own housing application.  Note: it may take up to 24 hours after a student completes their housing application for them to appear in the roommate selection tool. Log in to your MyHousing Account through e-Campus.
  • Log into MyHousing;
  • Choose “Room Selection” tab;
  • Choose “Roommate Selection” from the menu;
  • In the search box, find an incoming first-year student to add as a roommate;
  • Choose the name of the student who you want as a roommate;
  • Press the “Continue” button.
First-year students may ADD, DELETE or CHANGE roommate requests until the first-year housing application deadline. Only students who have completed their own housing application will appear in the search results. Roommate requests must be reciprocal. This means that the roommates you select must also select you and any other person in the room to create a “fully matched group”. To be certain that your roommate group is “fully matched” please check your MyHousing account.
